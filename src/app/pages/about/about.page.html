About SignalsMaster

Purpose

SignalsMaster is a mobile-first quiz app designed to help military and cadet personnel practice flag recognition, meanings, and signaling. Whether you're learning the basics or competing for the top spot, SignalsMaster provides an engaging way to master naval signal flags.

How to Use

  • Practice Mode: Learn at your own pace with no pressure. Perfect for beginners.
  • Best Signaller Mode: Compete globally and see how you rank against others.
  • Leaderboard: View top performers and track your progress.

Flag Categories

  • Letters (A-Z): NATO phonetic alphabet flags with their meanings
  • Numbers (0-9): Numeric signal flags
  • Pennants: Special pennant signals
  • Special Pennants: Command and control signals
  • Substitutes: Repeat and substitute flags
